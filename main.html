Peddi is an upcoming 2026 Telugu-language sports and crime action drama film, starring Ram Charan in the titular role, written and directed by Buchi Babu Sana, and featuring an ensemble cast including Janhvi Kapoor, Shiva Rajkumar, and Divyendu Sharma. The film is a period piece set in rural Andhra Pradesh, revolving around a spirited villager who unites his community through sports, specifically a cricket tournament, to defend their pride against a powerful rival.

Actors

Konidela Ram Charan, Janhvi Kapoor, Shiva Rajkumar, and Vijay Sethupathi, with music by A.R. Rahman.
